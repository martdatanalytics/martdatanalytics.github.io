Overview

MarDat Analytics Inc is committed to the development of the statistical sciences within and beyond the university. At the centre of our business model, we recognises that there is a need to provide quantitative and data analysis skills to students in other disciplines. We seek to cover the skills gaps in terms of data coding, analysis and results intepretation for the benefit of our clients. MartDat Analytics was established in 2017 to assist people meet the challenges of data handling and analysis that has become the nature of this Digital Age. Founded by Martin Canaan Mafunda, the company has assisted several students resolve their data analysis problems to the points of graduation. We are located in Thohoyandou, South Africa although our service provision is not limitedby geographical location.
