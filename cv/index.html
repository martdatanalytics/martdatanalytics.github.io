Mr Mafunda holds a Bachelors and a Masters degree in Applied Mathematics from the Midlands State University (MSU) in Zimbabwe and the University of Venda (UNIVEN) in South Africa. Prior to enrolling for his postgraduate degree with the University of Venda, Mr Mafunda worked for 4.5 years at Mashonaland Tobacco Company (MTC), first, as a Data Capture CLerk and later on as the Transport Supervisor and System and Administration Supervisor. For his MSc, Martin studied the mathematics behind the spread of HIV/AIDS and he investigated the multiscale nature associated with the spread of HIV. He developed a mathematical model and coded the model in python in a manner that allowed the comaprison of effectiveness of various HIV/AIDS interventions to be carried out. Besides, Martin is also holder of two executive certificates in Risk Management and Project/Programme Monitoring and Evaluation which he acquired through the Management Dvelopment Unit (MDU) Programme implemented by the University of Zimbabwe. Martin is well-versed with the following data analysis software tools: SPSS, Python Programming, R Statistical Programming, Open Refine for Data Cleaning and Excel Spreadsheets.
