Martin Mafunda

Mr Mafunda holds a Bachelors and a Masters degree in Applied Mathematics from the Midlands State University (MSU) in Zimbabwe and the University of Venda (UNIVEN) in South Africa. Prior to enrolling for his postgraduate degree, Mr Mafunda worked for 4.5years at Mashonaland Tobacco COmpany(MTC), first as a Data Capture CLerk and the as Transport Supervisor and Stystem and Administration Supervisor. For his MSc, Mr Mafunda studied the mathematics behind the spread of HIV/AIDS and he investigating the multiscale nature assocated with the spread of HIV. He developed a model and used python to code the system in a manner that allowed the comaprison of effectiveness of various HIV/AIDS interventions to be carried out.
